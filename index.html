About Us

Avogadro Labs is a computational biology startup focused on protein and enzyme engineering, monoclonal antibody (mAb) and peptide discovery, and biologics optimization.

We combine AI/ML, molecular modeling, docking, molecular dynamics simulations, and multi-omics analysis to accelerate therapeutic and industrial innovation.

Our Services

Protein & Enzyme Engineering

Design and optimize proteins for enhanced stability, activity, and industrial scalability.

mAbs & Peptide Discovery

Accelerated discovery and optimization of monoclonal antibodies and therapeutic peptides.

AI-based Toxicity Prediction

Predict toxicity profiles of small molecules and biologics using advanced ML models.

Protein-Ligand Modeling

Docking, molecular dynamics simulations, and interaction analysis for drug design.

NGS & Biomarker Discovery

Identify novel biomarkers using next-generation sequencing and omics integration.

Biologics Formulation Optimization

Enhance stability, delivery, and manufacturability of biologics.
